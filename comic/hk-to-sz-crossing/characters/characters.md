# Character Sheet — HK to Shenzhen Comic

## Main Character: Alex

**Role**: Protagonist — the Western tourist crossing the border

**Appearance**:
- Age: late 20s / early 30s
- Build: average, slightly lean
- Hair: short, warm brown, slightly wavy
- Skin: warm tan (#F7CFAE) — ligne-claire standard
- Eyes: expressive, dark brown, clear emotions
- Height: average
- Face: friendly, open, slightly round jaw — easily read across panels

**Clothing** (consistent across all panels):
- Light olive/khaki travel jacket (unzipped)
- Navy blue t-shirt underneath
- Dark jeans
- White sneakers
- Small backpack on back (gray)
- Rolling suitcase (dark navy, retractable handle) — present in panels 1–2

**Facial Expression Range**:
- Nervous-polite: panels 1, 4
- Curious-forward: panel 2
- Focused: panel 3
- Calm-routine: panel 4
- Relieved-excited: panel 5, cover

## Props
- Passport: dark blue/maroon cover, held open

- Suitcase: rolling navy suitcase with retractable handle

## ligne-claire Character Style Rules
- Uniform 2px black outlines on all elements
- Flat colors, no gradients
- Expressive but not exaggerated facial features
- Consistent proportions: 6-7 head heights
- Soft charcoal outline (#2D2D2D) instead of pure black
- Warm tone shift: all whites → cream, all grays → warm gray
